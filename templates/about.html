About WEXTheory

Theory practice, review, and exam words in one place

WEXTheory is built for learners preparing for Danish driving theory. It keeps the core flow simple: practice real-style questions, review mistakes, save difficult items, and use Exam Words 2026 when vocabulary gets in the way.

How the study format works

The main library follows a 25-question test format with a 20/25 pass target. After a test, learners can review answers, explanations, and saved questions instead of guessing what went wrong.

  • Starter Test 0 is available without registration.
  • Full access unlocks the complete practice library and Exam Words 2026.
  • Progress tools help learners return to the questions and terms they still need to repeat.

Why Exam Words is separate

Some learners know the traffic rule but lose time on exam vocabulary. Exam Words 2026 is kept as its own tool so it can be used quickly before or after tests, without mixing it into the question flow.
