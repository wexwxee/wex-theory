About

Built for people learning to drive in Denmark

WEXTheory was created to support theory study in Denmark. The platform uses structured practice questions, road-scene imagery, and review tools designed to help learners build confidence through repetition.

13
Structured Tests

13 structured practice sets, each with 25 questions covering a broad range of theory topics.

325
Question Library

Each question includes a road image and review feedback so you can understand the reasoning behind each answer.

How the study format works

Many learners prepare by working through 25-question multiple-choice sets and aiming for at least 20 correct answers. The question library covers topics such as road signs, traffic rules, vehicle awareness, and hazard perception.

WEXTheory uses the same 25-question structure across the main practice sets, so your study routine stays consistent and easy to track.
