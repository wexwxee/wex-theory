Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash). When you take tests, we store your answers and scores. If you send a contact message, we store your name, email, and message content.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide the service — authenticating you, saving your progress, and displaying your results. We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Cookies

We use a single authentication cookie ("token") to keep you signed in. This is a secure, HTTP-only cookie and contains only a reference to your session. No tracking or advertising cookies are used. Your theme preference is stored in your browser's localStorage.

4. Data Retention

Your account data and test history are retained while your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us.

5. Security

Passwords are stored as bcrypt hashes — we never store plaintext passwords. Session tokens are signed JWTs. We use HTTPS for all connections.

6. Third-Party Services

We use Google Fonts (Inter) for typography. Google may log requests made to their CDN according to their own privacy policy. No other third-party services are used.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access your personal data, request corrections, and request deletion. To exercise these rights, please contact us through the contact form.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please use our contact form.
