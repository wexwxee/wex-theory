Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 March 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password hash. We also store your test attempts, answers, saved questions, support messages, and any files you attach in support.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to run the service, verify accounts, reset passwords, save your progress, show your results, and reply to support requests. We do not sell your personal data.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We use an authentication cookie to keep you signed in. We also store UI preferences, such as theme choice and some temporary browser-side state, in local storage or session storage.

4. Email and Authentication Services

Verification emails and password reset emails are sent through Resend. Optional Google sign-in uses Google's authentication flow. If you choose those features, the related providers process the data needed to complete the action.

5. Security

Passwords are stored as secure hashes. We do not store plaintext passwords. Access to your account is managed through signed authentication tokens and HTTPS connections.

6. Data Retention

Your account and learning history remain in the system until they are removed. If you want your account and associated data deleted, contact support.

7. Third-Party Services Used

WEXTheory currently uses services such as Google Fonts for typography, Resend for transactional email delivery, and optional Google authentication. Access requests may also direct you to Telegram when you choose to contact us there.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, requests, or deletion enquiries, please use the contact page.
