Common Questions

What is included in the full practice library?
It includes all 13 tests, 325 questions, review explanations, saved questions, and test history inside your account.
Can I try the platform first?
Yes. Starter Test 0 is available for free without registration, so you can see the format before deciding whether you need the larger library.
How does access setup work?
Use the access button and follow the account-linked setup flow. Your account is then updated for the selected access period.
