Common Questions

What is included in the full practice library?
It includes all 13 tests, 325 questions, review explanations, saved questions, test history, and Exam Words 2026 for vocabulary practice.
Can I try the platform first?
Yes. Starter Test 0 is available for free without registration, and the Exam Words demo lets you preview the dictionary, cards, quiz, and audio flow.
How does access setup work?
Use the access button and follow the account-linked setup flow. Your account is then updated for the selected access period.
